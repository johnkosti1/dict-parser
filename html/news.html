good.• News is used with the singular form of words such as this and that: He was shocked when he heard this news. ✗Don’t say: these newsPrepositions with the news• If someone or something is on the news, they appear or there is a report about them on a television or radio news programme: The minister was on the 10 o’clock news.I saw the pictures of the crash on the news. • If someone or something is in the news, they are being discussed in newspapers and on news programmes: Education has been in the news a lot this week.COLLOCATIONSadjectivesgood newsHe’s feeling much better, so
