From Longman Dictionary of Contemporary Englishstrictstrict /strɪkt/ ●●○ S3 adjective (comparative stricter, superlative strictest)    1 STRICTexpecting people to obey rules or to do what you say OPP lenient  a strict teacherstrict about  This company is very strict about punctuality.strict with  The Stuarts are very strict with their children.2 STRICTa strict order or rule is one that must be
