also

From Longman Dictionary of Contemporary Englishalsoal‧so /ˈɔːlsəʊ $ ˈɒːlsoʊ/ ●●● S1 W1 adverb    1 AND/ALSOin addition to something else that you have mentioned → as well, too  Information is also available on women’s health care.  She sings beautifully and also plays the flute and piano.  The system was not only complicated but also ineffective.RegisterIn written English, avoid starting a sentence with also.
