willing

Word family (noun) willingness ≠ unwillingness (adjective) willing ≠ unwilling (adverb) willingly ≠ unwillingly

do something  How much are they willing to pay?quite/perfectly willing  I told them I was perfectly willing to help.2 → willing helper/volunteer/partner etc —willingly adverb  Sixty percent of voters
