called Fortuna.A brand is a type of product made by a particular company. You use brand to talk about products that you use every day, such as food and drink or cleaning products They sell many different kinds of coffee, including some of the less well-known brands. A make is the name of a particular product or the company that makes it, used especially about things such as electrical equipment and cars, but not about food or drink‘What make of car do you own?” “A Mercedes.’ A model is one particular type of car or machine from the various types that a company produces We produce a range of different computers, but this is our most popular model.
